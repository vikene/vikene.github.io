First Aid

In case of Emergency, how many of us know what to be done? What if we are in a no connectivity zone? In Emergency we need to act not google what has to be done! This app perfectly articulates the steps to be followed in case of an emergency. Every step is deeply explained and immense care has been taken to prevent flaws in the content. Here and there you have fabulous pictorial description which also facilitates to act accordingly. This app also allows you to store your personal credentials like blood group, age which would help doctors in case of an emergency and it also allows you to set an SOS number, which can be automatically called in case of an emergency. Thus First Aid app is the most vital tool for unexpected situations, and also the easiest way to aggregate all your critical information in case of an emergency.


Technical Details:


. It is compatable with almost all Nokia devices starting from Series 40 to ASHA series phones. Its User interface is designed using LWUIT and Coded in J2ME.
